Discover DRC Languages

Learn about the national and regional languages spoken throughout the Democratic Republic of Congo.

Lingala

Region: Kinshasa, Équateur, Mongala

Type: National Language

Widely used in music, military, and urban centers.

Swahili (Kiswahili)

Region: Katanga, Kivu, Eastern DRC

Type: National Language

Common in trade and spoken across East & Central Africa.

Tshiluba

Region: Kasai Region

Type: National Language

Language of the Luba-Kasai people.

Kikongo ya Leta

Region: Kongo Central

Type: National Language

Modern standardized Kikongo used for administration.
