Site Purpose

The Lubumbashi Chamber of Commerce website will serve as a central hub for businesses and entrepreneurs in Lubumbashi. The site will:

  • Provide information on local business events and workshops.
  • Offer networking opportunities for business owners.
  • Maintain a directory of local businesses.
  • Promote economic growth and entrepreneurship.

The website aims to support local businesses, attract new investments, and foster a connected business community.

Target Market

  • Small business owners and entrepreneurs in Lubumbashi
  • Corporate executives and investors interested in Lubumbashi
  • New residents or people considering relocating to Lubumbashi

Site Goals

  1. Improve member engagement by providing resources and networking opportunities.
  2. Attract new businesses and stimulate local economic development.
  3. Enhance the chamber’s visibility and credibility as a trusted authority in Lubumbashi business matters.

User Personas

  • Small Business Owner: Amina, 32, owns a boutique. Needs networking, business resources, and marketing support.
  • Corporate Executive: Thierry, 45, works at a logistics company. Interested in partnerships, investments, and networking.
  • New Resident: Joseph, 28, recently moved to Lubumbashi. Wants to explore business services, directories, and local events.

User Scenarios

  • A local business owner wants to join the chamber; they visit the site to find membership benefits, fees, and how to apply.
  • A resident wants to attend local events; they use the events calendar and register online.
  • An investor is considering relocating; they browse business directories, demographic info, and resources.

SEO Plan

  • Use keywords: "Lubumbashi businesses," "Lubumbashi Chamber," "business directory Lubumbashi," "Lubumbashi events."
  • Verify the website on Google Business Profile.
  • Obtain backlinks from member businesses to improve search rankings.
  • Embed Google Analytics to monitor website traffic.

Design Brief

  • Primary Color: #2E86AB (Deep Blue)
  • Secondary Color: #F4D35E (Yellow Accent)
  • Background Color: #F0F0F0 (Light Gray)
  • Text Color: #333333 (Dark Gray)
  • Font Family: Poppins, sans-serif

Site Map

  1. Home – Overview, news, featured businesses, upcoming events
  2. About Us – Mission, history, staff, chamber initiatives
  3. Membership – Benefits, types of membership, join application
  4. Business Directory – List of local businesses by category
  5. Events – Calendar, event details, registration links
  6. Resources – Guides, templates, government links, reports
  7. News/Blog – Updates, success stories, economic news
  8. Contact – Contact form, location map, social media links

Wireframes

Desktop: Navigation bar at top, hero image with call-to-action, featured businesses, upcoming events, footer with contact info.

Mobile: Hamburger menu, scrollable hero section, collapsible business listings, events, footer with essential links.
