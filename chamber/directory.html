About Us

The Lubumbashi Chamber of Commerce website serves as a central hub for businesses
and entrepreneurs in Lubumbashi. We provide information on local events and workshops,
offer networking opportunities, maintain a directory of local businesses, and promote economic
growth and entrepreneurship, supporting a connected and thriving business community.
